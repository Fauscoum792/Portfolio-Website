Hollow Knight gives a unique look upon the fall of a civilisation and the variety of life that rose to dwell in its ruins and feast on its corpse.
The colour motives of each biome lead to a dual purpose of making each area easily distinguishable and grounding the main themes of each location Dusty grey for the once well trodden path of the forgotten crossroads. The verdant life overtaking the ruins in Greenpath and the decaying corpse of the once opulent City of Tears.
The lands of Hollownest are populated by its many denizens, benign or otherwise. From the forgotten founder god who even now corrupts the bugs who forsook him, reducing a grand civilisation to base savagery with a light that once brought life, to a beetle who loves maps and his wife.
